# FIFA Fantasy 2026 - Rules Reference Guide

Welcome to your FIFA Fantasy 2026 reference guide. Use this file to quickly verify squad restrictions, transfer mechanics, scoring points, and chip usage.

---

## 🏆 Squad & Nation Limits

Your squad must consist of exactly **15 players**:
*   **2** Goalkeepers (GK)
*   **5** Defenders (DEF)
*   **5** Midfielders (MID)
*   **3** Forwards (FWD)

### 💰 Budget Constraints
*   **Group Stage (Rounds 1–3):** $100 Million
*   **Knockout Stage (Rounds of 32 onwards):** **$105 Million** (An extra $5 Million is added to accommodate premium knockout-stage players)

### 🌍 Nation Selection Limits
To prevent single-nation dominance, the maximum number of players you can select from the same country changes as the tournament progresses:

| Stage | Matches | Max Players Per Country |
| :--- | :--- | :---: |
| **Group Stage** | Rounds 1–3 | **3** |
| **Round of 32** | Knockout Round 1 | **3** |
| **Round of 16** | Knockout Round 2 | **4** |
| **Quarter-finals** | Knockout Round 3 | **5** |
| **Semi-finals** | Knockout Round 4 | **6** |
| **Final** | Final & Third-Place Playoff | **8** |

## 🔄 Transfers & Knockout Deadlines

Transfers let you swap underperforming or eliminated players. Make sure to finalize all transfers before the deadline for each round (typically the kickoff of the first match of that round).

### 🚀 Free Transfers Per Round
*   **Round of 32 (Current):** **Unlimited Free Transfers** 
*   **Round of 16:** **4** Free Transfers
*   **Quarter-finals:** **4** Free Transfers
*   **Semi-finals:** **5** Free Transfers
*   **Final / Third-Place:** **6** Free Transfers

> [!WARNING]
> **Point Deductions:** Each additional transfer made beyond your allocated free transfers in any round costs **-3 points** from your overall score.
> *Note: Free transfers do NOT roll over to the next round.*

## 🔁 Substitutions & Captaincy Changes

FIFA Fantasy allows highly interactive, manual adjustments *during* a live round, as long as matches are played over consecutive days.

### 🏃 Manual Substitutions
During a round, you can manually replace a player in your starting XI who has already played with a player from your bench who has **not yet played** in that round.
*   **Subbing out:** Choose a player who got low points (e.g., 1 or 2 pts).
*   **Subbing in:** Choose a bench player whose match is scheduled for a later day.
*   **Restriction:** Once you substitute a player out, their points for that round are permanently lost and cannot be retrieved. If a player is sent off (red card), they cannot be substituted.

### 👑 Captaincy Rotation
You can change your captain during a live round to another player in your squad whose team has not yet played.
*   The double-points bonus will transfer to the new captain.
*   The previous captain will revert to their normal single-points score.
*   **Restriction:** Once you captain a player who has already played, you cannot captain them again in that round if you switch away from them.

## 📊 Point Scoring Matrix

Players earn (or lose) points based on their on-field statistics.

### ⚽ Core Actions
*   **Appearance (Up to 60 minutes):** +1 pt
*   **Appearance (60+ minutes):** +1 pt (Total +2 pts for playing full/most match)
*   **Assist:** +3 pts
*   **Penalty Saved (GK):** +3 pts
*   **Winning a Penalty:** +2 pts
*   **Conceding a Penalty:** -1 pt
*   **Yellow Card:** -1 pt
*   **Red Card:** -2 pts
*   **Own Goal:** -2 pts

### 🥅 Position-Specific Points

| Action | GK | DEF | MID | FWD |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Goal Scored** | **+9** | **+7** | **+6** | **+5** |
| **Clean Sheet (60+ mins)** | **+5** | **+5** | **+1** | — |
| **Saves (Every 3 saves)** | **+1** | — | — | — |
| **Conceding (2nd goal & onwards)** | **-1** | **-1** | — | — |

> [!NOTE]
> **Conceding Goals:** The first goal conceded does not cost any points. Goalkeepers and Defenders lose **1 point for each additional goal** conceded after the first one (i.e. 2nd goal, 3rd goal, etc.).

### 🌟 Performance & Scouting Bonuses
*   **Goal from direct free-kick:** +1 pt (All positions)
*   **Midfielder Tackle Bonus:** +1 pt for every 3 successful tackles
*   **Midfielder Chance Creation:** +1 pt for every 2 key chances created
*   **Forward Shot Bonus:** +1 pt for every 2 shots on target
*   **Scouting Bonus:** **+2 pts** if a player is owned by **fewer than 5%** of all managers and scores 4 or more points in a match.

## ⚡ Boosters (Chips) Reference

You have five unique Boosters available for the entire tournament. You can only use **one booster per round**, and each booster can only be used **once**.

1.  **🃏 Wildcard**
    *   **Effect:** Make unlimited free transfers for a single round. 
    *   *Usage Tip:* Useful in the group stages or if your squad suffers major injuries. (Note: Since Round of 32 already gives unlimited free transfers, the Wildcard cannot be used in this round).

2.  **🧑‍🤝‍🧑 12th Man**
    *   **Effect:** Add a 12th player to your squad for one round. This player will score points but has no budget limits and does not count towards nation limits.
    *   *Constraint:* The 12th Man cannot be captained or substituted.

3.  **👑 Maximum Captain**
    *   **Effect:** At the end of the round, the player in your starting XI who scored the highest number of points is automatically awarded the Captain's double points.
    *   *Usage Tip:* Best used in a round where you have multiple superstars playing, eliminating the risk of captaining the wrong player.

4.  **📈 Qualification Booster**
    *   **Effect:** Adds **+2 points** to any player in your starting XI who successfully progresses to the next round (or wins the final).
    *   *Constraint:* Only available during the knockout stages (Round of 32 onwards).

5.  **🛡️ Mystery Booster: Clean Sheet Shield**
    *   **Effect:** Goalkeepers, defenders, and midfielders in your starting XI who play 60+ minutes will keep their clean sheet points (+5 for GK/DEF, +1 for MID) even if their team concedes **one goal**. They will only lose their clean sheet if their team concedes 2 or more goals.
    *   *Usage Tip:* Perfect to play in rounds with tight, defensive matches or when you are heavily invested in premium defenders playing tough opponents.
