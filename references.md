# 🔗 FIFA Fantasy 2026 - Resources & References

This directory contains a curated list of high-quality websites, analysis portals, video creators, and communities where the agent (or manager) can check for player projections, predicted lineups, team reveals, chip strategies, and real-time news updates.

---

## 🏆 1. Official Game & Rules
*   **[Official FIFA World Cup Fantasy Game](https://play.fifa.com/fantasy/team)**
    *   *What to use it for:* Managing your team, checking direct transfer deadlines, monitoring live player price fluctuations, verifying official rosters, and tracking overall leaderboards.
*   **[Official FIFA Fantasy Rules & Help](https://play.fifa.com/fantasy/help)**
    *   *What to use it for:* Verifying edge-case scoring rules (e.g., tackle/shot bonuses, clean sheet exceptions) and chip/booster behavior.

## 📊 2. Dedicated Analysis & Strategy Websites
These sites provide comprehensive stats, expert articles, projections, and stage-specific guides.

*   **[Fantasy Football Scout (FFScout) - World Cup Section](https://www.fantasyfootballscout.co.uk/world-cup)**
    *   *Key Features:* The most trusted authority in fantasy football. Offers detailed match previews, predicted lineups, expert opinion pieces, and comprehensive stats tables.
    *   *Best for:* Lineup predictions, fixture difficulty ratings, and injury/suspension news.
*   **[Fantasy Football Hub](https://fantasyfootballhub.co.uk)**
    *   *Key Features:* Features "Ultimate Guides" for major international tournaments. Offers detailed projections, expected points (xP) models, and interactive planning tools.
    *   *Best for:* Mathematical projections and optimized drafts.
*   **[ALLABOUTFPL](https://allaboutfpl.com)**
    *   *Key Features:* Extremely active with free, in-depth articles. Produces excellent differential picks, booster strategy logs, and captaincy rotation charts for each round.
    *   *Best for:* Free, detailed stage-by-stage planning guides.
*   **[RotoWire - Soccer](https://www.rotowire.com/soccer/)**
    *   *Key Features:* Specialized statistical articles focusing on player floors and ceilings. Great breakdown of chip mechanics.
    *   *Best for:* Identifying niche players who generate high points from recoveries, tackles, and shots on target.
*   **[Fantasy Football Geek](https://www.fantasyfootballgeek.co.uk)**
    *   *Key Features:* Simple, text-heavy, but highly analytical previews written by experienced fantasy managers.
    *   *Best for:* Clean sheet chances analysis and comparing different expert draft teams.
